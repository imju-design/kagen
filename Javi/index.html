Javi Cang

Photographer

Among the many hats he wears, Javi (He/Him) is a visual storyteller and adventurer. Finding a home in the mountains, he has had the opportunity to witness both the beauty and fragility of many wild places. From tracing the glacial source of the Ganges River in the Himalayas to stand-up paddling the entire length of the Pasig River, his photographs and stories resonate with the reality of our natural world and its delicate beauty. It is these experiences that draw him to lend a voice to the environment. He spoke at the Youth Climate Strike in Manila and had the opportunity to narrate a film for Prince William's conservation endeavour - The Earthshot Prize.
