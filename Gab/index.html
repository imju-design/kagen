Gab Mejia

Photographer

Gab Mejia is a conservation photographer and environmental storyteller. His work covers stories on the intertwining relationships and issues of nature, people, and wildlife. He is a National Geographic Explorer and a World Wide Fund for Nature Philippines Board of Trustee, where his work has taken him to document the mountains, marshes, forests, and islands around the world. Through his photographs, he hopes to inspire a new generation of conservation photographers in the Philippines and beyond.
