Angelo Mendoza

Photographer, Filmmaker

Angelo Mendoza (He/Him) is a professional photographer and filmmaker based in Metro Manila, Philippines. Over the course of his career shooting travel, lifestyle, and commercial projects, he has been published worldwide through a variety of global brands such as Netflix, and The New York Times. Whether on an assignment or not, he strives to capture stories of humanity using his distinct style of surreal, visual storytelling. Through collaborative projects like partnering with WWF-PH on fundraising events, and documenting the effects of natural disasters for World Food Programme, Angelo hopes to shine light on environmental issues that plague underrepresented communities, and give a voice to those people who donâ€™t have the proper avenues for themselves.
