Kara Pangilinan

Visual Artist, Muralist

Kara Pangilinan (She/Her) is a 27 year old visual and muralist known mostly for her intricate black & white body of work. Over the years, she has learned to channel her emotions and experiences into the lines, shapes and patterns of her work, turning her art into an instrument of release as well. She has been invited to share her story and her process of using art as a form of self-expression, at various events around the Metro and even in a global conference held in Sydney, Australia.
