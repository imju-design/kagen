Sara Erasmo

Photographer, Mixed Media Artist

Sara Erasmo (She/Her) is a still life photographer and mixed media artist based in Metro Manila, Philippines. Inspired by the natural world and the magic in the inanimate, her work is a play of textures, colors, and mediums. Her intuitive, experimental approach reframes the seemingly mundane in a raw yet delicate light.
